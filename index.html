    3D modelling a city app

    The challenge: To complete a 48 hour design sprint, wireframing and designing a city app from scratch.

    Method: For this project I used Blender to construct a 3D city scape of Kuala Lumpur. I imported satellite map and terrain data from Google, and used this as a base. I then applied a high rise texture on the buildings.

    Tools: Blender, Figma, UX/UI design
